✎ Identity Tip of the Day

Check for any application programming interfaces (APIs) in your environment that don't enforce rate limiting or authentication. APIs are often overlooked in audits, creating unintentional entry points for attackers. List them and prioritize those with sensitive access or data exposure for immediate remediation.
