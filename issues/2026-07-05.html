✎ Identity Tip of the Day

For those deploying identity wallets, conduct an internal audit of current authentication methods. Pay special attention to apps that still rely on static credentials and rank them by access level. Transition high-access applications to support more dynamic, multi-factor methods like those offered by digital wallets, reducing exposure from legacy systems.
