✎ Identity Tip of the Day

Review API tokens and OAuth settings to check for overly broad scopes or expired tokens not yet deactivated. Focus on integrations with high value or sensitive systems - these tokens could be a breach waiting to happen, and removing unused connections is often overlooked.
