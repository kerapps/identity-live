✎ Identity Tip of the Day

Run a check for any unused OAuth tokens across all third-party integrations that have access to your sensitive data. Revoke tokens that haven't been used in over six months. This reduces exposure from forgotten or poorly monitored connections and helps mitigate breaches like Klue's.
